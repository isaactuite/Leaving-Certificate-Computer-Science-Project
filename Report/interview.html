Sleep Apnea Interview

Q: How frequently do you experience symptoms of sleep apnoea, such as snoring, gasping for breath, or interruptions in breathing during your sleep?

A: I experience symptoms like that a lot, I regularly wake myself up, and your mother is always complaining that she cant get any sleep because of my snoring.

 

Q: Are you currently using any form of treatment for your sleep apnoea, such as a CPAP machine or other breathing assistance devices?

A: No, Ive never got around to it, even though my doctor told me that I should look into it.

 

Q: Have you ever monitored your own heart rate or blood oxygen levels during sleep?

A: I had a Fitbit a few years, ago, it measured my sleep quality and heartrate level.

 

Q: How aware are you of the potential health risks associated with untreated sleep apnoea, such as cardiovascular problems and decreased oxygen saturation levels during sleep?

A: Im not exactly aware of the risks, but I imagine there could be many potential consequences.

 

Q: Would you be interested in a device that monitors your sleeping heartrate and blood oxygen level, and then provides you with advice on how to deal with the symptoms?

A: Yes, I would.

 

Q: In your opninion, would such a device improve your level of wellbeing?

A: Yes, I think it would, it might help me decide if I need further medical treatment.
